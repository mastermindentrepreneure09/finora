About Finora

What is Finora?

Finora is an independent finance education platform created to help students and beginners understand personal finance and investing in a simple and practical way.


Our Mission

Our mission is to remove confusion, fear, and misinformation around finance by providing clear explanations and educational tools that anyone can understand.


What We Offer

Finora provides finance blogs, calculators, and learning resources focused on SIPs, mutual funds, interest, and long-term financial planning.


Who We Are

Founded by Sairaj Kashid, Finora is built with a student-first mindset and focuses purely on education rather than financial advice or product promotion.
